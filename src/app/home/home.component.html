About Us

Welcome to the Musomatic website. We are a small student run business from Lornshill Academy who offer personalised music products. Our aim as a student company is to provide our customers with the highest quality product and buying experience. We believe everyone has that one song that reminds them of a special time or perhaps a special place so we want to turn that memory into a personalised product fit for you.
